About Us

Sud & Go is a mobile car detailing service run by two brothers who believe in quality, convenience, and making your ride shine — inside and out. We bring expert detailing to your driveway, saving you time while delivering professional-grade results.

🧼 Interior Detail – What’s Included

  • 🛞 Vacuum seats, carpets, and trunk
  • 🛞 Floor mats vacuumed and steam cleaned
  • 🛞 Carpet and fabric shampooing
  • 🛞 Fabric seat steam clean or leather clean and condition
  • 🛞 Clean door and trunk jambs
  • 🛞 Clean and condition all interior vinyl, plastic, and leather
  • 🛞 Streak-free glass cleaning

🚗 Exterior Detail – What’s Included

  • 🛞 Car shampoo bath
  • 🛞 Premium hand wash with microfiber mitts
  • 🛞 Power washing of vehicle exterior
  • 🛞 Bug removal
  • 🛞 Wheel wells cleaned
  • 🛞 Tires scrubbed and shined
  • 🛞 Exterior glass cleaned
  • 🛞 Protective wax finish
